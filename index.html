Security Controls

          
Designed for safe finance operations

        

        

          
AuthenticationDemo login models role-based access, MFA state, session timeout, and lockout after repeated failures.

          
Data privacyEmail addresses are masked in audit logs and sensitive secrets stay in environment variables.

          
Prompt injectionStructured invoice fields are escaped in the browser and deterministic templates prevent data from becoming instructions.

          
Database safetySQLite tables use typed fields, timestamps, and auditable generated email records.

          
Email governanceDry-run status prevents accidental sends; legal-stage accounts are flagged for human review only.

          
Production hardeningUse server-side auth, hashed passwords, HTTPS, CSRF protection, SPF, DKIM, DMARC, and RBAC approvals.
